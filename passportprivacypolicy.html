1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Passport Photo Maker (the “App”), published by Rise of the Rebels (“we”, “us”, or “our”), handles information when you use it. By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide

The App does not ask you to create an account, sign in, or submit personal details such as your name, email address, or phone number. We do not collect this information.

Information collected automatically

The App itself does not collect analytics or usage data. However, third-party services that help us run and monetize the App (see Section 5) may automatically receive certain device information, such as a device advertising identifier, IP address, device model and operating system version, and general usage signals used to serve and measure ads.

3. Your photos & camera

Creating a passport, visa, or ID photo requires access to your device camera and/or your photo gallery. Any image you capture or select is processed entirely on your device to crop, resize, adjust the background, and lay out the photo.

  • Your images are not uploaded to our servers, because the App has no backend server.
  • We do not store, view, share, or sell your photos.
  • Saved photos are kept in your device's local storage or gallery, under your control, and can be deleted by you at any time.

4. How information is used

The limited information described above is used only to:

  • provide the App's core feature — generating ID/passport photos on your device;
  • display advertisements that keep the App free;
  • maintain, fix, and improve the App's stability and performance.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your photos with anyone. The only data shared is the device/advertising data collected directly by the third-party services in Section 5, in accordance with their policies and applicable law.
